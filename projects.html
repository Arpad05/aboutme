Malware Analysis

I'm currently working on a malware analysis software to compare th chi-square and Shannon entropy value in order to flag possible collisions.
By doing so, it is easier to differentiate classical random files such as jpg and possibly encrypted or compressed files.
In sum, all comes to compare the output of Shannon's entropy : $$H(X) = - \sum_{i=1}^{n} p_i \log_2 p_i$$ and chi-square's probability : $$\chi^2 = \sum_{i=1}^{n} \frac{(O_i - E_i)^2}{E_i}$$ .
